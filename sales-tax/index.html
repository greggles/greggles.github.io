Sales and use taxes

Do you need to collect sales and use taxes?

In Colorado, the sales tax rate of 2.9% and any additional local sales taxes, apply to the sale of goods, not services. You must collect these taxes on each sale and then pay that amount to the state if:

  • you have a business location, inventory, or employees making retail sales in Colorado; or
  • you are an out-of-state retailer with annual retail sales into Colorado of more than $100,000 in the current or previous calendar year.
